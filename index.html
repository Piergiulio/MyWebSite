The Darkness! Peak Game Of The Season!

New Game! Old Era!

As advertised by many as a call to old games RPG's from the '80s/'90s and as a big fan of retrogames in general I had to buy and try it myself!

I will be complete honest with you, I never heard about the Twincuz Production before and as soon as I found myself inside their website I realized that they are a fairly new group of developper and The Darkness is actually their very first game!

So I was in for a challenge: Could be the birth of a star or the complete detruction of my expetations!


So on a dark and rainy day, I downloaded the game and after it has being installed I was ready to begin this new adventure!

And let me tell you that worth was the wait! In not time I've being catapulted in a old style RPG which brought me back when I use to wait queing outside the game shop to buy the newest Final Fantasy or Dragon Quest!

It felt a lot like a Final Fantasy pre Play Station era! And you can see that those games "played" an important part on the inspiration for The Darkness!

The Game overall is very good. The plot is very solid and I appreciated a lot the charachters development inside the story, expecially the main charachters which are essentially five!

But alongside them there are a lot of secondary characters, each of them with their personality and different skills! And a lot of side quest which can give us more powerfull skills and weapons!

So A very well Done to Twincuz Production for this Gem and keep up The Hard Work!

Thomas Holed
